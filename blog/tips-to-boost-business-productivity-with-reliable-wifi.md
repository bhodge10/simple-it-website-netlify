## 6 Signs Your Network Needs Help

Wondering if your Wi-Fi is really holding you back? Watch for these red flags:

- Speed: Slow upload, download, and streaming performance

- Lag: Delays between clicking and things happening

- Dropouts: Wi-Fi signal keeps cutting out

- Jitter: Calls sound garbled or video skips

- Coverage: Dead zones around the office

- Security: Unknown devices sneaking onto your network
