## **Benefits of Microsoft Forms for Business Decisions**

Microsoft Forms makes data collection simple and accessible for everyone.

- **Easy to Use:** A clean drag and drop builder makes it simple to create forms without special training.

- **Microsoft 365 Integration:** Forms connects directly to Teams, SharePoint, Excel, and Power Automate, which helps drive data based decisions.

- **Real Time Data:** Results arrive instantly and can be shown in charts or graphs that Forms generates automatically.

- **Mobile Friendly:** Forms adjust smoothly to phones, tablets, or laptops so people can respond from anywhere.

## **Unlocking Business User Features**

Forms includes a wide range of features designed with everyday business users in mind.

### **Customizable Forms & Templates**

You can quickly spin up surveys for customer satisfaction, event sign-ups, employee feedback, and much more.

### **Question Types**

Choose from a variety of question formats including:

- Multiple choice

- Short text or long text

- Rating scales

- Likert scales

- Date or time pickers

- File uploads

### **Sharing Options**

Forms can be shared with internal staff or external users. Access is based on Microsoft 365 credentials and Forms can be embedded in web pages, emails, or Microsoft Teams tabs.

### **Data Analysis**

Because Forms integrates so easily with Excel, it becomes simple to organize and review data to support business decisions.

## **Work Scenarios**

Forms can support a wide range of tasks across departments:

- **Human Resources:** Employee surveys, onboarding checklists, exit interviews

- **Marketing:** Customer feedback, event follow up

- **Training:** Course sign up, knowledge checks

- **IT:** Help desk tickets, asset inventory, basic system requests

## **Microsoft 365 Integration**

Because Forms is built right into Microsoft 365, it works smoothly with the tools many Northern Kentucky organizations use every day.

### **Excel**

Each Form automatically creates an Excel file where responses are stored and ready for review.

### **Power Automate**

You can build automated workflows based on Form responses, which can help reduce repetitive steps and improve response times.

### **SharePoint and Teams**

Forms can be placed directly inside Teams or SharePoint pages, creating an easy and collaborative space for data collection.

## **Microsoft Forms Tips**

- Set clear objectives so every question has a purpose.

- Use branching to skip unnecessary questions based on a user‚Äôs answers.

- Offer anonymous response options when gathering sensitive feedback.

- Limit open ended questions so results are easier to organize and understand.
