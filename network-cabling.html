Frequently Asked Questions

What type of cabling do you recommend for businesses?

For most businesses, we recommend Cat6A cabling‚Äîit supports 10 Gigabit speeds and is future-proof for the next 15+ years. For longer runs or higher-density environments, we install fiber optic. We assess your specific needs and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

How long does a cabling project typically take?

A small office (10-20 drops) typically takes 1-2 days. Medium projects (50-100 drops) take 3-5 days. Larger projects vary, but we always provide a detailed timeline before starting. We schedule around your business hours to minimize disruption.

Can you improve our WiFi without running new cables?

Sometimes, yes. We can optimize access point placement, upgrade equipment, and adjust channel configurations. However, enterprise WiFi still needs wired backhaul to each access point for best performance. We'll do a free site survey and recommend the most cost-effective approach.

Do you handle permits and building codes?

Yes. We're familiar with Kentucky and Ohio building codes and handle all coordination. Our installations meet or exceed TIA/EIA standards. We also provide full documentation and certification testing on every drop we install.

What's included in a network assessment?

Our free network assessment includes a site survey of your current cabling and equipment, WiFi heat mapping to identify dead zones and interference, bandwidth testing at multiple points, and a written report with prioritized recommendations. There's no obligation‚Äîjust honest information about your network.
