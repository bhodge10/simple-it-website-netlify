Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between managed IT and break-fix support?

Break-fix IT means you call when something breaks, then pay hourly rates while you wait for help. Managed IT is proactive‚Äîwe monitor your systems 24/7, prevent problems before they occur, and respond in 15 minutes when issues arise. You pay a fixed monthly fee and get unlimited support.

How quickly do you actually respond?

Our guarantee is 15 minutes for critical issues, but our actual average last quarter was 11 minutes. For remote issues, we're often logged in and working within 5-8 minutes. For onsite support, we're never more than 30 minutes away in Northern Kentucky.

What if I already have an internal IT person?

We work alongside your internal IT staff to extend their capabilities. We handle monitoring, maintenance, security, and emergency support‚Äîfreeing your IT person to focus on strategic projects. Many clients tell us we've made their IT person 3x more productive.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We operate on month-to-month agreements because we believe we should earn your business every month through great service, not trap you in a contract. Our 98% retention rate proves this approach works‚Äîclients stay because they want to, not because they have to.

How much does managed IT cost?

Pricing depends on your number of users, devices, and specific needs. Most clients pay $99-199 per user per month. We'll provide exact pricing during your free assessment. Most businesses find they save money compared to break-fix IT while getting significantly better service.
