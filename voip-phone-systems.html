Frequently Asked Questions

How much can we save switching to VoIP?

Most businesses save 30-50% compared to traditional phone lines. One of our clients cut their monthly phone bill from $580 to $200 by switching to a cloud-based VoIP system. Exact savings depend on your current setup, number of lines, and features needed.

Will VoIP work with our existing internet connection?

In most cases, yes. VoIP requires about 100 Kbps per concurrent call. We'll assess your bandwidth and network quality during a free evaluation. If upgrades are needed, we can handle that too as part of our network services.

What happens to our phone system if the internet goes down?

We configure automatic failover to mobile devices, so calls route to cell phones if your internet drops. We also recommend redundant internet connections for businesses where phones are critical. You'll never miss a call.

Can we keep our existing phone numbers?

Absolutely. Number porting is standard with every VoIP deployment. We handle the entire process with your current provider. Porting typically takes 2-4 weeks, and we set up temporary forwarding so you never miss calls during the transition.
