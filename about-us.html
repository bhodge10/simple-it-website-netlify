How We Got Here

The story of why Simple IT exists

The Problem We Saw

Back in 2010, Northern Kentucky businesses were stuck with two bad options: huge IT companies that treated them like ticket numbers, or solo techs who disappeared when things got complicated.

Response times were terrible (4+ hours was "normal"), prices were unpredictable, and nobody seemed to care about preventing problems before they happened.

We knew there had to be a better way.

What We Built Instead

Simple IT was founded on a radical idea: what if IT support actually worked the way businesses needed it to?

Fast response times (guaranteed 15 minutes). Transparent, predictable pricing. Real relationships with your team. Proactive monitoring that prevents fires instead of just putting them out.

Most importantly: no long-term contracts. If we're doing our job right, you'll want to stay. If we're not, you shouldn't have to.

Why "Simple" IT?

Because technology should be simple. Not dumbed down‚Äîsimple. It should work when you need it, be secure without being complicated, and cost what you expect it to cost.

We handle the complexity so you don't have to think about it. Your email just works. Your files are backed up. Your team can work from anywhere. And when something does go wrong (because let's be honest, technology isn't perfect), we fix it fast.

That's what "simple" means to us.
