Frequently Asked Questions

How many cameras does my business need?

It depends on your facility size, layout, and security goals. Most small offices need 4-8 cameras. Warehouses and retail spaces typically need 8-16. We do a free on-site survey and provide a camera placement plan that covers all entry points, high-value areas, and blind spots.

Can I view cameras remotely from my phone?

Yes. All our camera systems include mobile apps for iOS and Android. You can view live feeds, review recorded footage, receive motion alerts, and even talk through two-way audio cameras‚Äîall from your phone, tablet, or computer, anywhere with internet access.

How long is video footage stored?

Standard setups store 30 days of continuous recording. We can configure longer retention (60, 90, or 180 days) based on your needs and compliance requirements. Storage options include on-site NVR, cloud storage, or hybrid solutions.

Do you install access control and badge entry systems?

Yes. We install and manage complete access control systems including badge/key card entry, keypad systems, and biometric readers. These integrate with your camera system so you get video verification of every access event.

What happens if a camera goes down?

With our managed security camera plans, we monitor camera health 24/7. If a camera goes offline, we're alerted immediately and dispatch a technician. Most issues are resolved remotely within the hour. For hardware failures, we provide next-business-day replacement.
